Make a Platformer

In my work with TAG Labs we table at various local cons and workshops. It can be hard to split time between entertaining the kids and explaining workshop details to the parents. During Awesome Con in 2016 I used the default assets in Unity to make this very basic tool to make your own platformer. And kids eat it up, each year for awesome con we have other projects we try to entertain kids with but they keep coming back to this little prototype.
